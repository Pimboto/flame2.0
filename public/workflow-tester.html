Endpoints Disponibles

GET /workflows - Lista todos los workflows
GET /workflows/executions - Historial de ejecuciones
POST /workflows/execute - Ejecuta un workflow
GET /workflows/instances/:id - Obtiene el estado de una instancia
PUT /workflows/instances/:id/suspend - Suspende un workflow
PUT /workflows/instances/:id/resume - Reanuda un workflow
DELETE /workflows/instances/:id - Termina un workflow
